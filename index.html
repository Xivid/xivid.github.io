Biography

I am a senior undergraduate student at Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT), Harbin, China. I joined the Computer Science Elite Class, and IBM Technology Center (a.k.a. FoOTOo Lab), both in my freshman year. I am currently working as a research intern in the Systems Research Group at Microsoft Research Asia, mentored by Dr. Lintao Zhang. I was an exchange student at the Department of Computer Science, The University of Hong Kong in Fall 2015. I have been writing codes for more than ten years.

My research interests are in the broad computer systems area, particularly in distributed systems, cloud computing, operating systems and programming languages. I am extremely excited about hacking into low-level system issues. Watch my demo of RISC-CPU with Multiple Interruption on FPGA if you are interested (sorry for the Chinese narration and subtitles, it won't affect understanding, though).
