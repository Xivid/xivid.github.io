Biography

I am a senior undergraduate student at Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT), Harbin, China. I joined the Computer Science Elite Class, and IBM Technology Center (a.k.a. FoOTOo Lab), both in my freshman year. I am currently working as a research intern in the Systems Research Group at Microsoft Research Asia, mentored by Dr. Lintao Zhang. I was an exchange student at the Department of Computer Science, The University of Hong Kong in Fall 2015. I have been learning to code for more than ten years.

My research interests are in the broad computer systems area, particularly in distributed systems, cloud computing, operating systems and programming languages. I am extremely excited about hacking into low-level system issues and building real systems.
