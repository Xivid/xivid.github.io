Biography

I am a senior undergraduate student at Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT), China. I am a member of the Computer Science Elite Class, and IBM Technology Center (a.k.a. FoOTOo Lab). I am currently working as a research intern in the Systems Research Group at Microsoft Research Asia, mentored by Lintao Zhang. I was an exchange student in Fall 2015 at the Department of Computer Science, The University of Hong Kong.

My research interests are in the broad computer systems area, particularly in distributed systems, cloud computing, operating systems and programming languages.
